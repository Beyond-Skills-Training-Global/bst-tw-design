OUR CORE PRINCIPLES

WE ARE ON A MISSION TO EMPOWER COACHES AND ATHLETES. ANY WORTH WHILE MISSION REQUIRES STRONG
CORE PRINCIPLES.

1

CONSISTENCY

It is no secret to coaches that youth athletes globally are missing out on consistent athleticism training. General athleticism training builds long-term movement libraries, prevents injuries, and translates into resilient sports athletes. Yes. A few athletes do have access to one-on-one coaching sessions that address general athleticism.

READ MORE
2

QUALITY

There are endless coaching resources available. The problem nowadays is not the lack of information but instead, it's the lack of information filtering. We are committed to doing the hard work of compiling, filtering, and designing exercises and training routines intelligently so that they are implementable in most youth sports training plans.

READ MORE
3

SIMPLICITY

We are approaching this a little bit from the decathlon perspective: keeping it stupid simple. A registered coach puts together a plan and athletes get it on their phones immediately. Athletes don't even have to register. No need to printout exercise routines, or spend time youtube-ing exercise videos to later send out to athletes.

READ MORE
4

AFFORDABILITY

We are here to offer every youth athlete an opportunity to have consistent, wide-range general athleticism exercises available at their fingertips. All-yearlong. From year-to-year. We want to see athletically prepared youth athletes in every corner of the world, ready to build long and beautiful stories in their favorite sports.
