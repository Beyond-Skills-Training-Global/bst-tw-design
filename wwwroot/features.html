BST.LIBRARY

EXERCISE VIDEOS
TRAINING ROUTINES
TRAINING PLANS

We have filmed and categorized our own high quality exercise video library ranging from very simple to quite advanced exercises. Currently BST.COACH has nearly 700 exercise videos that hits all aspects of general athleticism, including strength, power, speed, agility, mobility, balance and more. Since our primary audience are youth athletes, we have also made sure the exercises are fun and engaging. lots of crawling, skipping, jumping and rolling...

The vast majority of our exercises are body weight or require minimal equipment, such as jump rope, workout mat, resistance band or a stopwatch. Some of the more advanced exercises require dumbbells, hurdles, boxes,medicine balls or physio balls.

Our videos are purposely made in 8-12 second bite-size format to minimize time waste during training.

Our library is constantly growing

2

MY LIBRARY

EXERCISE, ROUTINES, PLANS

We encourages coaches to create there own unique library

Coaches can add unlimited amounts of videos from YouTube or Vimeo links. BST training routines and plans can be edited and saved in my Library.

My Library feature makes us accessible to all coaches including those out side of youth sports.In fact, we are currently the most affordable and one of the simplest-to-use platforms on the market as we do not require registrations for our athletes.

3

EXPLORE TRAINING VIDEOS

After lots of trial and error we decided that people like scrolling these days. Scroll away because our videos conveniently open by themselves just like in TikTok or Instagram.

Once you’ve found what you like, click Add and you have your exercise demo added to the plan. I hope it doesn’t create a new form of addiction for coaches.

4

SEND PLANS

This is where we shine. After two years of testing effectiveness of the platform, we came to conclusion that simple-stupid is the way to go. coach clicks send which immediately creates a homepage for your group. That simple.

5

AUTOMATE TRAINING PLANS

Automating training helps coaches plan ahead, saves time and money. Coaches can upload multiple plans in a row and add a date range when their athletes would be able to see each plan. Often it comes in handy when the training group is split and some athletes are forced to train alone for extended periods of time, for example, while recovering from an injury.

6

GROUP PAGE

Group page is a personalized extension from our platform where the coach can place everything he or she wants their athletes to see. It’s protected by a secure key coach hands out to the group athletes. It’s the homeroom for each training group.

7

TEAM SHARING

This feature allows Team Account users to share resources within the organization or between coaches. Often this is the missing link in sports institutions. Many resources but no unity in overarching training approach. Team sharing solves it.

8

OTHER FEATURES

Every small feature we have on our platform was designed out of the necessity to solve personal coaching struggles. For example, the ability to hide/unhide training days from athletes, copy plans, replace exercises/routines, add alternative exercise or drag & drop training units were all designed to maximize effectiveness and save time for coaches as they create training for their athletes.
