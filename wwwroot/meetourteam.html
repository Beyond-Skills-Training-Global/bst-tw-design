REINIS KREGERS

FOUNDER

Reinis, a native of Latvia is someone who is always on a mission whatever the task. he earned B.S. in Kinesiology from Kansas State University in 2016, and gained coaching wisdom through out his 18 year decathlon career from world renowned coaches in the U.S., Czech Republic and Latvia.

Currently Reinis and his wife reside in Prague where he still continue straining in the decathlon under the former world record holder, Roman Šebrle, as well as coaching youth athletes in Czech baseball and basketball.
