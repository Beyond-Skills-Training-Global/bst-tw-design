EXTENDED BST LIBRARY: EXERCISES, ROUTINES, TRAINING PLANS

Search through and apply nearly 700 exercise video demos (and growing), 150+ pre-made exercise routines, covering all essential athleticism aspects, and 30+ weekly training plan templates. Our exercise videos are designed in a way to serve coaches and athletes in real-time during training. Each video is a short, bite-size 8-10 second demonstration to remind athletes of the exercise.

BUILD YOUR OWN LIBRARY WITH EXERCISES, ROUTINES, AND TRAINING PLANS

Add unlimited amounts of videos through YouTube or Vimeo links and create your own exercise library. This comes in handy, especially if you are a sports coach with some favorite sport-specific exercises that you want to include in your athletes' training plans. You can also edit the BST training routines & plans and save these resources in your personal library.

CREATE, SEND & AUTOMATE TRAINING PLANS

Filter exercises or training routines by category and apply the right ones directly to your training plan. Once you are satisfied with the plan, send it to your athletes by sharing a link. Athletes will only have to plug in a password to see your training plan. No registrations are needed for your athletes. In some situations, there could be a need to create several plans in a row - to save time, you can simply apply the appropriate dates to each plan, and they will show up and change automatically under your athletes' training link (group page).

UP TO 5 TRAINING GROUPS & 20 ATHLETES PER GROUP

Create serve up to 5 separate training groups simultaneously. Apply an unlimited amount of training plans for each group. You can always delete your old training groups and create new ones. Each training group page can be accessed by up to 20 athletes from various devices. If you need to serve more athletes per group, please let us know, and we will find a solution.
