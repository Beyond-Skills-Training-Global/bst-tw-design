OUR CORE PRINCIPLES

WE ARE ON A MISSION TO EMPOWER COACHES AND ATHLETES. ANY WORTH WHILE MISSION REQUIRES STRONG
CORE PRINCIPLES.

1

CONSISTENCY

It is no secret to coaches that youth athletes globally are missing out on consistent athleticism training. General athleticism training builds long-term movement libraries, prevents injuries, and translates into resilient sports athletes. Yes. A few athletes do have access to one-on-one coaching sessions that address general athleticism. But most athletes don't. Our platform provides consistency and opportunity to both: coaches who can always be present at training sessions, but especially those who cannot always provide individual attention to prepare movement foundations for sports.

2

QUALITY

There are endless coaching resources available. The problem nowadays is not the lack of information but instead, it's the lack of information filtering. We are committed to doing the hard work of compiling, filtering, and designing exercises and training routines intelligently so that they are implementable in most youth sports training plans. We are concerned with building only the highest-grade training resources for youth coaches and athletes. International competitive and coaching experiences have helped us see what works.

3

SIMPLICITY

We are approaching this a little bit from the decathlon perspective: keeping it stupid simple. A registered coach puts together a plan and athletes get it on their phones immediately. Athletes don't even have to register. No need to print out exercise routines, or spend time youtube-ing exercise videos to later send out to athletes. We have filmed a whole library of bite-size exercise videos that can be easily added to any training plan. Our platform is actually quite a complex web with many advanced features, yet we have worked very hard to show you only what's necessary to accomplish the tasks at hand. Until you ask for more.

4

AFFORDABILITY

Affordability is part of our mission. We are here to offer every youth athlete an opportunity to have consistent, wide-range general athleticism exercises available at their fingertips. All-year long. From year-to-year. We want to see athletically prepared youth athletes in every corner of the world, ready to build long and beautiful stories in their favorite sports. We will fight so that no youth athlete in the world lacks the opportunity to receive good training resources. We will make sure you can afford us no matter where you come from.
